About

never ever give up

CancerDB is a public domain blog assembling key datasets on cancer for dependable models. The focus is on cancer, cancer preventions and treatments, but the database also includes datasets on closely connected things--from cancer types to organizations and more.

CancerDB is for two groups of people:

  1. Cancer researchers. CancerDB is organized big data to help you create great new preventions and treatments and improve existing ones. Benefit from the experiences of millions that have gone before you. If you are researching something and can't find what you need here, open an issue and explain what you need.
  2. Those battling cancer right now. CancerDB provides a data-driven view of the cancer universe, to demystify the world of cancer for you, and provide sound strategic and tactical advice to help you get and stay healthy. If you have a question not answered by the data here, open an issue and explain what you need.

Open source

All data and source code used to generate this site are on GitHub and public domain.

History

CancerDB.com was purchased by Breck Yunits on September 3, 2022.
