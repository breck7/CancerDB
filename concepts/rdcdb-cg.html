id rdcdb-cg name RDCDB type registry standsFor Registre des cancers de Brazzaville description The registry was founded in 1995. It is located in the records section of the Service de Carcinologie et Radiotherapie of the CHU de Brazzaville, which also serves as the records store of the department.The Registry is financed by the Ministry of Health and the Marien NGouabi University.
