Endometrial Carcinoma

edit

Endometrial Carcinoma is a type of Uterine Cancer

Subtypes of Endometrial Carcinoma include Poorly Differentiated Carcinoma of the Uterus, Uterine Adenosquamous Carcinoma, Uterine Carcinosarcoma/Uterine Malignant Mixed Mullerian Tumor, Uterine Clear Cell Carcinoma, Uterine Dedifferentiated Carcinoma, Uterine Endometrioid Carcinoma, Uterine Mesonephric Carcinoma, Uterine Mixed Endometrial Carcinoma, Uterine Mucinous Carcinoma, Uterine Neuroendocrine Carcinoma, Uterine Serous Carcinoma/Uterine Papillary Serous Carcinoma, Uterine Undifferentiated Carcinoma
