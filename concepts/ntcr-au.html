../code/conceptPage.scroll id ntcr-au name NTCR type registry website https://health.nt.gov.au/data-and-research/Innovation-and-research/northern-territory-cancer-registry appeared 1981 standsFor The Northern Territory Cancer Registry description The Northern Territory Cancer Registry (NTCR) was established in 1981 by the Northern Territory Department of Health and Families to collect information about cancer in the NT. The Cancer (Registration) Act was passed in 1988 to provide a legislative basis for the Registry, including protecting the privacy of people with cancer. The Act was last updated in 2011. country Australia
